# Resume PDF

Place your resume PDF here as `resume.pdf`.

The filename must be exactly: `resume.pdf`

It will be downloadable from both:
- The hero "Download CV" button  
- The navbar "CV" button
- The mobile nav "Download CV" link

When deployed on Vercel, files in the `/public` folder are served at the root URL.
So `public/resume.pdf` becomes `https://ckmasangcay.vercel.app/resume.pdf`.
